# AI SUMMARY: Forge (Neural Laboratory)

## What is this subsystem?
A model-building environment inside EVO for designing and training domain-specific AI.

## Why does it exist?
To allow the EVO platform to "manufacture" its own specialized intelligence instead of just consuming general-purpose LLMs.

## Interaction Map
- Managed by **ForgeSessionManager**.
- Results can be exported as **GGUF** models for local use.
- Future: Darwin will evolve the model architectures.

## Status Note
Currently a **Reasoning Skeleton**. Training is simulated; real tensor backends are planned for Milestone 1.
